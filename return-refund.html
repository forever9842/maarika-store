Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026

1) Return Eligibility

  • Return requests must be made within 24-48 hours of delivery (as applicable).
  • Product must be unused, with original packaging.
  • Return is accepted only in case of damaged/defective product or wrong item delivered.

2) Unboxing Proof

For damage/defect claims, an unboxing video/photo proof may be required.

3) Refund Process

  • Refund will be processed after verification.
  • Refund mode: UPI/Bank transfer (or original payment mode if possible).
  • Refund timeline: 3-7 working days after approval.

4) Non-Returnable Items

  • Items damaged due to customer misuse
  • Used products
  • Products without original packaging

5) Contact

To request a return/refund, contact Maarika Store on WhatsApp with your Order ID and issue details.
